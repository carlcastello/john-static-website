Description

The main level features the magnificent formal living room with stone face wall and 12 ft ceilings. The kitchen boasts Maple wood cabinets and Granite counter tops. The customized main entrance door is made of Narra wood. The staircase's planks and handrails are made of Narra wood and Stainless steel railings. The spiral staircase planks are made of Narra wood and Stainless steel handrails and side rails.

All Bedrooms on the 2nd floor has its own sliding glass door access to each balcony, sliding glass (mirror) closets with built-in cabinets & drawers. The Terrace on the 2nd floor is very spacious with 55.7 sq. m. floor area. The Porch and Balconies all around the house are stainless steel railings

Ground floor – 12ft high customized ceiling. 2nd floor – 10ft customized ceiling. 3rd floor Attic – 9ft ceiling. The Floor Slabs (ground floor up to the 3rd floor) – 12 inches thickness reinforced concrete. The Hollow blocks used for the exterior walls of the house were 6 inches thick; in addition, the mortar/finishing (both sides) and stone wall cladding make the total thickness of the exterior walls 10 inches thick.

The perimeter reinforced concrete fence wall height = 2.5m plus 1m steel grating on the top of the wall. The overall height of the fence is 3.5m. The dimensions of the corner columns/post = 300mm X 300mm X 3.75m (4nos). The dimensions of the center columns/post = 250mm X 250mm X 3.75m (33nos). The hollow blocks used for the fence wall was 5 inches thick; in addition, the mortar/finishing (both sides) make the total thickness of the fence wall 8 inches thick.
